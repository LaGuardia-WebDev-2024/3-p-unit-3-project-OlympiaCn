Recipe #1

  • Time: 20 mins
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredients Quantity
Indomie, Buldak, Shin Ramen 1-2 packs depending on hunger
Hot water 2-3 cups
sesame & scallion
(optional)		 reasonable amount

Step 1: Pour your water into a pot and boil at medium heat for about 5 minutes.

Step 2: Once boiling, add in your ramen.

Step 3: Strain your noodles after 3-4 minutes.

Step 4: Add in your sauce packet, scallions, and sesame.

Recipe #2

  • Time: 45 mins
  • Serves: 3 person
Ingredients Quantity
Leftover/Day old rice however much is left
Taiwanese Sausages 2-3 sticks
Ground pork 1/2 a pound
Garlic 3 cloves
sesame & scallion
(optional)		 A little
peas, carrots, corn reasonable amount
Soy Sauce, Salt, White and Black pepper, Msg 3 cloves

Step 1: Add oil to your pan when hot.

Step 2: Add in your minced garlic and chopped scallions.

Step 3: When fragrant add in your slices sausages and minced pork.

Step 4: Add in your minced vegetables.

Step 5: Add in your rice and mix well.

Step 6: Once mixed add soy sauce to taste, about 2 tablespoons.

Step 7: Add in white and black pepper as well as msg and stir.

Step 8: You're done!!
